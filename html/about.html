About QuizNova

Welcome to QuizNova, a dedicated interactive learning platform designed specifically for students to master core subjects. Our purpose is to help you self-assess your knowledge in Programming (C++), Discrete Math, ICT Basics, Cyber Security, Functional English, and Islamic Studies.

This project was built as an Academic ICT project to demonstrate modern web development practices while keeping things simple, friendly, and easy to use.

How to Play

Follow these simple steps to start testing your skills.

1

Select a Topic

Choose a subject from the Home page (e.g., C++, Discrete Math).

2

Answer Questions

Questions appear one by one. Read carefully and select the best option.

3

Finish & View Score

After 10 questions, submit your quiz to see your final score and performance badge.
